# MY favorite movie: *MAZE RUNNER*

## Overview
The *Maze Runner* is a thrilling sci-fi action film set in a dystopian future. It follows a group of teens trapped in a mysterious maze with no memory of how they got there. Each day, runners try to map the ever-changing labyrinth in hopes of finding a way out. As secrets are uncovered and alliances are tested, the fight for survival grows more intense. The film combines mystery, suspense, and action in a high-stakes setting.

## Main character
**Thomas:** is the brave and curious protagonist. 
**Teresa:** The first and only girl to arrive in the Glade, she shares a mysterious connection with Thomas.
**Newt:** A calm, level headed second in command who becomes one of Thomas’s closest allies.
**Minho:** The lead Runner, known for his speed, bravery, and sarcastic sense of humor.
**Gally:** A strong-willed Glader who believes in following the rules and sees Thomas as a threat to their fragile order.
**Alby:** The first boy to arrive in the Glade and the original leader.

## Why It Stands Out:
[x]Fast-paced and full of suspense.
[x]Mysterious dystopian world.
[x]Themes of survival, friendship, and memory loss.
[x]Intriguing plot twists and action scenes.

I love the movie *The Maze Runner* because it blends mystery, action, and friendship in a high stakes environment. The maze itself is a fascinating puzzle, and the story keeps me guessing. The characters are relatable and courageous, and their struggle to survive and escape creates nonstop tension and emotional moments. It’s a story about resilience, teamwork, and challenging the unknown.
